Frequently Asked Questions
How quickly do you respond to support requests? +
General enquiries are answered within 1 business day. Security vulnerability reports receive an acknowledgement within 24 hours. Enterprise customers on Professional and above plans receive priority support with a 4-hour response SLA during business hours.
Can I request a live demo? +
Yes — select "Sales & Pricing" in the form above and mention you'd like a demo. We'll set up a 30-minute call to walk through the platform, discuss your use case, and answer any technical questions.
Do you offer reseller or MSSP programmes? +
Yes. We have a structured MSSP/reseller programme with volume discounts, white-label options, and dedicated API endpoints. Contact enterprise@swarmhawk.com for our partner pack.
How do I report a bug or security vulnerability? +
For security vulnerabilities, please email security@swarmhawk.com with a description of the issue, steps to reproduce, and impact. We follow coordinated disclosure and credit researchers who responsibly report valid findings. For general bugs, use the contact form above with topic "Technical Support".
Where can I find API documentation? +
Full API documentation is available at swarmhawk.com/docs.html. It covers all endpoints, authentication, response schemas, rate limits, and code examples in cURL, Python, and JavaScript.
