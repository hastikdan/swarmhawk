What powers SwarmHawk

SSL/TLS & Infrastructure

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What exactly does SwarmHawk scan? ▼
SwarmHawk runs 23 automated security checks on any domain — including SSL/TLS validity, DNS configuration (SPF, DMARC, DKIM), open ports via Shodan, data breach exposure (12B+ accounts), blacklist status across VirusTotal, Spamhaus, URLhaus and Google Safe Browsing, CVE vulnerabilities in detected software, typosquat monitoring, dark-web credential intelligence (ransomware, infostealers, Telegram channels), and an AI-generated threat summary. Every scan queries live data — no cached results.
Do I need technical knowledge to use it? ▼
No. SwarmHawk is built for business owners and compliance teams, not just security engineers. Enter your domain, get a plain-English report with a risk score (0–100), colour-coded findings, and a prioritised action list. The AI threat summary translates every finding into language your board or legal team can understand.
How is SwarmHawk different from free tools like SSL Labs or MXToolbox? ▼
Free tools check one thing at a time. SwarmHawk runs 23 checks in parallel from a single scan, cross-references results across 17 intelligence sources, calculates a unified risk score, and generates an AI briefing with remediation steps. It also tracks your domain over time, sends monthly PDF reports, and provides NIS2/DORA audit evidence — none of which free tools offer.
Is my data safe? Who can see my scan results? ▼
Scan results are stored securely in your private account — no other user can access them. We query public-facing data only (the same data any attacker can see), so we never need credentials or access to your systems. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. All API traffic is encrypted over HTTPS.
What does the $50/year plan include, and can I cancel? ▼
The Annual plan ($50/year per domain) includes monthly automated rescans, email alerts on new critical findings, downloadable PDF reports suitable as NIS2 / DORA audit evidence, AI threat briefings, and unlimited domains. You can cancel at any time — no long-term contract. The $10 one-time option gives you a single full scan and report with no recurring charge.
